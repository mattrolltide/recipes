Ingredients

  • Butter [1 cup]: You'll need butter to saut√© the vegetables.
  • Vegetables: For this recipe you'll need onions (2 cups chopped), celery (2 cups chopped), and mushrooms (12 ounces sliced).
  • Parsley [1/4 cup]: This will add some pop and flavor.
  • Bread [12 cups]: You should use slightly stale bread which you'll need to cut into cubes.
  • Spices and seasonings: This recipe calls for salt (1 & 1/2 teaspoons), dried sage (1 & 1/2 teaspoons), poultry seasoning (1 teaspoon), dried thyme (1 teaspoon), dried marjoram (1/2 teaspoon), and ground black pepper (1/2 teaspoon).
  • Broth [4 & 1/2 cups]: You can use store bought broth or home-made chicken broth, the choice is yours.
  • Eggs [2 large eggs beaten]: The eggs will help hold everything together and will give it some moisture.

Steps

  1. Step 1: Melt the butter in a skillet over medium heat. Cook and stir the onion along with the celery, mushrooms, and parsley in the butter. Do this until util the ingredient soften. This should take around 5-8 minutes.
  2. Step 2: Place bread cubes in a very large mixing bowl. Spoon cooked vegatables over the bread cubes. Season with salt, sage, poultry seasoning, thyme, marjoram, and pepper. Then pour enough broth to moisten and mix in the eggs. After all this is done, place in a slow cooker.
  3. Step 3: Cover and cook on High for 45 minutes then reduce the heat to low and cook for 4 to 8 hours.
  4. Step 4: Enjoy!
